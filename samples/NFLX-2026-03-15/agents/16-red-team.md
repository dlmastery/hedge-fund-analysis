## Key Concerns (Bearish Tilts):

### 1. Insider Selling ($287M RED FLAG)
Sarandos, Peters, Hastings selling simultaneously in 90 days with ZERO offsetting buying. Historical base: insider selling precedes stock decline 65-70% of time (2-6 month window). **Strongest bearish signal available.**

### 2. Consensus Crowded (81% Bullish)
Sell-side analyst consensus 81% bullish despite 15% YTD decline and $287M insider selling. Implies street view too optimistic; consensus reversal risk high. When 80%+ bullish in decline environment, contrarian flip likely.

### 3. Kelly Criterion Negative
Assume bull case 40% probability ($138 upside), base 30% ($108.94), bear 30% ($68 downside):
- Expected value: (0.40 × $138) + (0.30 × $108.94) + (0.30 × $68) = $110.68 vs. $94.96 current
- Kelly criterion: (0.40 × 0.45) - (0.30 × 0.28) / (0.45 + 0.28) = 5.2% = BUY
- HOWEVER: If bull case probability only 30% (vs. 40%), expected value = $103.63; Kelly = -2% = AVOID
- **Sensitivity high to bull case probability weighting.**

### 4. Engagement Decline Accelerating
Engagement down 3-5% YoY in mature markets. If this accelerates to 5-8% YoY, churn rises within 2 quarters. LTV/CAC compression (9.4x → 8.0x) impacts unit economics.

### 5. Tariff Risk Extreme
60% international content spend exposed to 10-15% tariff shock (Trump administration 2025-2026 policy risk). Binary outcome: (1) Tariffs implemented = $1.0-1.5B EBITDA headwind, or (2) Tariffs negotiated away = no impact. BINARY RISK with 40-50% probability of realization.
