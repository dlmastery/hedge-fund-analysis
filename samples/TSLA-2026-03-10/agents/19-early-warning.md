- **Trend:** Accelerating — BYD +25% YoY, Tesla +8% YoY
- **Implication:** Tesla no longer #1 by volume. Price competition intensifying.
- **Lead Time:** 6-12 months

### 2. China Delivery Collapse
- **Signal:** Wait times contracted from 8-12 weeks (2025) to 1-3 weeks (Feb 2026)
- **Trend:** Inventory glut forcing aggressive discounting
- **Implication:** Demand-supply imbalance signaling weakness, not strength
- **Lead Time:** 3-6 months

### 3. Insider Selling (Net -$26.8M in 90 Days)
- **Signal:** $26.8M net selling, ZERO open market buys by directors/executives
- **Trend:** 90-day consecutive selling with no counterbalancing purchases
- **Implication:** Insiders see deterioration ahead; signaling is real
- **Lead Time:** 3-9 months (insiders typically sell 6-9 months before public warning)

### 4. Massive Workforce Reductions
- **Signal:** 1,700 Berlin layoffs + 14,000 prior (15,700 total headcount reduction = ~14% workforce cut)
- **Trend:** Cost cutting accelerating after earlier rounds (Texas, Nevada)
- **Implication:** Margin compression expected; demand not supporting prior headcount assumptions
- **Lead Time:** Immediate (already executing)

### 5. NHTSA FSD Safety Investigation
- **Signal:** 80+ collision complaints, formal investigation opened
- **Trend:** Regulatory pressure mounting on autonomous features
- **Implication:** FSD revenue at risk (subscription base could churn; regulatory delay likely)
- **Lead Time:** 6-24 months regulatory resolution

### 6. Lithium Cost Spike
- **Signal:** Lithium prices surged to 138,000 CNY/tonne (+1.47% MoM)
- **Trend:** Supply tightening from Australia/Chile cutoffs
- **Implication:** COGS pressure + margin compression (EV industry-wide)
- **Lead Time:** 3-6 months impact on financials

### 7. Auto Loan Rejection Rate at 15.2% (Highest Since 2008)
- **Signal:** Credit tightening across auto sector. Loan rejection spike = demand destruction signal
- **Trend:** Rising rates + unemployment fears driving credit pullback
- **Implication:** Tesla buyer pool narrowing; demand elasticity real
- **Lead Time:** 3-6 months

### 8. Musk Distraction
- **Signal:** CEO attention split across SpaceX IPO, xAI rebuild, DOGE, Twitter litigation
- **Trend:** Escalating non-Tesla commitments during critical FSD/Cybertruck execution phase
- **Implication:** Operational risk for execution on autonomous/energy roadmap
- **Lead Time:** Ongoing

### 9. Glassdoor Culture Deterioration
- **Signal:** Employee satisfaction 3.5/5, work-life balance 2.8/5 (lowest major auto company)
- **Trend:** Cascading departures of engineering talent
- **Implication:** FSD development velocity at risk; quality issues emerging
- **Lead Time:** 6-12 months
