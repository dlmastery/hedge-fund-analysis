## Net Assessment

CEO ecosystem has **mixed impact on Tesla valuation: approximately net neutral to slightly negative when risk-adjusted.** Positive synergies ($5B risk-adjusted) are real but modest relative to Tesla's scale ($580B market cap = 0.9% value boost). Negative costs ($5B risk-adjusted) nearly offset, creating zero net synergy impact. However, **key-person risk dominates:** if Musk departs unplanned, Tesla loses 25–40% near-term value ($120–200B destruction). This is a 1–2% annual risk (8–12% over 5 years) that should be quantified as -$1.2–2B annual cost-of-capital. **Recommendation: (1) Establish formal succession plan and disclose publicly; (2) reduce Musk attention fragmentation by appointing operating CEOs to xAI/Neuralink; (3) implement key-person insurance; (4) monitor related-party transactions quarterly; (5) quantify ecosystem synergies annually to investors.**
